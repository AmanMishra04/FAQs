What Is A Blog/Blog writing?

Blogs, are online journals that are updated frequently, sometimes even daily. An update,(also called an entry or a post) is usually quite short, perhaps just a few sentences, and readers can often respond to an entry online. People who write blogs are commonly called "Bloggers".

What Is A Coding Language?

A programming language is: ‘an artificial language built to allow someone to give instructions to a computer’. Computers can’t understand English, Hindi, or Chinese, and even though humans can technically learn binary (the base language of computers), almost none do.
